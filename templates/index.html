Analysis


While comparing the dogs time spent in a shelter against various characteristics such as age, breed and gender, we were able to determine whether or not they were still available for adoption.Using a logistic regression, our machine learning model is just over 82% accurate.

Conclusion

Adopting a dog requires a lot of consideration on the part of the owner. While some dog characteristics have perceived influence over likelihood of being adopted, after the analysis one stood out as being a clear factor: age. The average life expectancy for dogs is 10-13 years. The longer they spend in a shelter, the odds go down they will find a forever home.
